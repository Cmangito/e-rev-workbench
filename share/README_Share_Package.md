- `E-Rev_Workbench_Portfolio_Brief.html`
- `E-Rev_Workbench_Portfolio_Case_Study.md`
- `E-Rev_Workbench_Audit_Notes.md`
- Optional: a zipped copy of the app folder if you want them to open the prototype locally

## App Opening Instructions

If sending the app folder directly:

1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open `index.html` in a browser.
3. Use **Realistic sample** to load the fictitious demo dataset.

The app runs locally in the browser. CSV and sample-data workflows do not require a server. Excel parsing may need internet access for the optional spreadsheet parser.

## Positioning

Describe E-Rev as a portfolio prototype inspired by a workflow idea, not as a finished product. The strongest framing is:

> I built this to explore how deterministic environmental spreadsheet review could be paired with AI-supported drafting in a way that stays auditable.

## Important Caveat

The sample data is fictitious, the criteria are placeholders, and trend labels are first-pass screening aids. The prototype is not for regulatory use.
